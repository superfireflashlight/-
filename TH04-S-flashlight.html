TH04-S Professional LED Flashlight

The TH04-S is our flagship professional-grade flashlight designed for industrial applications, outdoor adventures, and emergency services. With an impressive 3000 lumens output and 500-meter beam distance, this flashlight delivers exceptional performance in the most demanding environments.

Superior Brightness & Range

Equipped with the latest CREE XHP70.2 LED technology, the TH04-S produces an intense 3000 lumens beam that can reach up to 500 meters. Whether you're navigating dark industrial facilities or searching remote areas, this flashlight provides the illumination you need.

Rugged & Reliable

Constructed from high-strength aircraft-grade aluminum alloy with military-grade hard anodizing, the TH04-S is built to withstand harsh conditions. It's IP68 waterproof rated (submersible up to 2 meters) and impact resistant up to 1.5 meters.

Advanced Features:

  • 5 programmable lighting modes: High, Medium, Low, Strobe, and SOS
  • Intelligent temperature control prevents overheating during extended use
  • Digital battery indicator shows remaining power at a glance
  • USB-C fast charging with power bank function to charge other devices
  • Lockout function prevents accidental activation during transport

The TH04-S comes complete with a high-capacity 5000mAh rechargeable battery, USB-C charging cable, lanyard, holster, and user manual. Backed by our 5-year warranty and 30-day money-back guarantee, this is the last flashlight you'll ever need to buy.
