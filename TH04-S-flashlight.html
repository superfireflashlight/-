Product Detailed Description

The SUPERFIRE TH04-S represents the perfect combination of portability and performance in a compact flashlight design. Weighing just 155 grams with dimensions of 135×28×28mm, this lightweight flashlight delivers an impressive 1200 lumens of brightness with a 280-meter beam distance, making it ideal for everyday carry and outdoor adventures.

Powerful Illumination

1200 lumens maximum output with advanced CREE LED technology for exceptional brightness in a compact form factor

USB-C Rechargeable

Built-in 1800mAh lithium battery with USB-C fast charging for convenient power replenishment anywhere

Ultra Lightweight

Only 155 grams with compact dimensions, perfect for everyday carry without fatigue during extended use

5 Lighting Modes

High/Medium/Low/Strobe/SOS modes with memory function that remembers last used setting for convenience

The TH04-S features a precision-machined aluminum alloy body with military-grade hard anodizing for superior durability. The tactical tail switch provides instant access to all lighting functions, while the anti-slip knurling ensures a secure grip in any conditions. The integrated USB-C charging port eliminates the need for separate chargers, allowing convenient recharging from power banks, laptops, or wall adapters.

Whether for daily carry, outdoor activities, emergency preparedness, or professional use, the SUPERFIRE TH04-S delivers exceptional performance in a compact, lightweight package that fits easily in your pocket, backpack, or tool kit.
