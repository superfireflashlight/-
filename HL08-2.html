How to Use

1

Power On/Off

Press and hold the switch button for 2 seconds to turn on/off the headlamp

2

Mode Selection

Single click to cycle through 5 modes: Low → Medium → High → Strobe → SOS

3

Focus Adjustment

Pull or push the lamp head to adjust between spot and flood beam patterns

4

Tilt Adjustment

Rotate the lamp head up to 90° to direct the beam where you need it most

5

Charging

Connect Type-C cable to charge. LED indicator shows charging status and battery level
